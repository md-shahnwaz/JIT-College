Director Message:

As Director of Jahangirabad Educational Trust Group of Institutions, it is my pleasure to address all of you. You are aware that our vision is to provide quality education at affordable price. Towards this end a number initiative is being undertaken.

  • We are tying up with a funding institution to provide loans to desirous students at nominal EMI.
  • Hiring of quality faculty is currently underway.
  • Seminars, field trips, industry exposure, projects, placements are being enhanced and fine tuned.
  • We are now affiliated to district cricket and football associations and our teams will be able to participate up to state levels selected players may even go to represent at the national level.
  • Infrastructure is being built to provide world class cricket and football facilities.
  • An indoor sports complex is being planned.
  • Coaches of repute are being hired.
  • All our affiliations are in order.
  • Alumni Interactions are on the increase.

Overall, I can assure all of our students and parents, that JETGI will provide you with a holistic experience to include education and sports. The students who pass out from our institutions will be all rounder’s, capable of rubbing shoulders with the very best from industry or any other field, in the nation & abroad.

Major Gen Sandeep Singh (Retd.)

Director, Jahangirabad Educational Trust Group of Institution

JIT believe in quality and affordable education by leveraging all tools of modern education.
